Yuzhou District

Yuzhou District of Yulin City, located in the southeast of Guangxi, is the seat of the People's Government of Yulin City, and the political, economic and cultural center of Yulin City. Yuzhou District is the political, e conomic and cultural center of Yulin City.

Honorary Title

  • In October 2018, it was selected as "2018 Top 100 National Investment Potential Areas".
  • In April 2020, it was selected as one of the top 100 counties and cities in China for medical services in 2020.
  • In December 2020, it won the 2019 Guangxi High-quality Development Advanced Urban Area.

Geographical Position

Yuzhou District is located between 22°32′2"-22°49′16" north latitude and 109°59′27"-110°18′40" east longitude, bordering Wuzhou City in the southeast, Guigang City in the northwest, and Beihai and Qinzhou City in the west. It is 566 kilometers away from Shenzhen and 420 kilometers away from Guangzhou, which is the junction of South China and Southwest China, and is a convenient passage for southwest and east to the sea. The total area of the region is 464 square kilometers.

Fumian District

Fumian District, the second district of Yulin City, southwest of Yulin City Center.

Honorary Title

  • On July 15, 2021, it was selected into the list of the fourth batch of counties that meet the standards for water-saving social construction by the Ministry of Water Resources.
  • On September 8, 2021, it was selected into the pilot list of rooftop distributed photovoltaic development in the whole county (city and district) of the comprehensive department of the National Energy Administration.

Geographical Position

The center of Fumian District is located at 22°35′2" north latitude and 110°02′42" east longitude, southwest of Yulin city center. It borders Yuzhou District to the northeast, Xingye County to the northwest, Bobai County to the south, and Luchuan County to the southeast. The total area is 829 square kilometers.

Beiliu City

Beiliu City, formerly known as "Guangdong-Guitongqu" and "Ancient Tongzhou", was once "rich in the party" in history, known as "Little Foshan" and "Golden Beiliu". Beiliu has a long history and is named after the Guijiang River flowing from south to north.

Honorary Title

  • The hometown of the world's copper drum king
  • The hometown of lychee in China
  • 2021 Top 100 Counties and Cities in Western China

Geographical Position

Beiliu City is located in the southeast of Guangxi, adjacent to the Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area, known as the "Guangdong-Guangxi Tongqu", named because the Guijiang River flows from south to north. The total area is 2457 square kilometers.

Rong County

Rong County, belonging to Yulin City, Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, is located in the southeast of Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region and the east of Yulin City.

Honorary Title

  • In 2017, Rong County was selected as the third batch of pilot areas to support migrant workers and other personnel to return to their hometowns and start businesses in combination with new-type urbanization.
  • In February 2020, Rong County was selected as the third batch of advantageous areas for agricultural products with Chinese characteristics.
  • On October 12, 2021, Rong County was selected as the fifth batch of national ecological civilization construction demonstration zones.

Geographical Position

Rong County is located in the southeast of Guangxi, east of Yulin City, between 110°15'~110°53' east longitude, 22°27'-23°07' north latitude, Cen Xi City, Wuzhou City in the east, Tengxian County in the northeast, Beiliu City in the west, Guiping City in the northwest, Xinyi City and Beiliu City in Maoming City, Guangdong Province in the south, and Tengxian County and Pingnan County in the north. The total area is 2257.39 km².

Luchuan County

Luchuan County belongs to Yulin City, Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, and is named after the six rivers in the county: Jiuzhou River, Mima River, Shahu River, Rongjiang River, Low Yang River and Qinghu River.

Honorary Title

  • In 2012, Luchuan County, Guangxi won the "National Pig Transfer County" award
  • On June 30, 2020, it was selected into the "Second Batch of Revolutionary Cultural Relics Protection and Utilization Counties List".
  • On June 29, 2021, it was identified as "Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region Rural Revitalization Key Assistance County".

Geographical Position

Luchuan County is located at the southeast end of Gui, is the southern gate of Guangxi, bordering Lianjiang and Huazhou cities in Guangdong to the south, 138 kilometers from the county seat to Zhanjiang; It is bordered by Yuzhou District to the north, Beiliu City to the east, and Bobai County to the southwest. The total area is 1154 square kilometers.

Bobai County

Bobai County, known as Baizhou in ancient times, belongs to Yulin City, Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region. It is the largest gathering place of Hakka in Guangxi and the largest Hakka settlement in the world.

Honorary Title

  • On February 2, 2019, it was named the "Hometown of Chinese Folk Culture and Art" in 2018-2020 by the Ministry of Culture and Tourism.
  • On June 30, 2020, it was selected into the "Second Batch of Revolutionary Cultural Relics Protection and Utilization Counties List".
  • On September 22, 2020, it was selected as one of the "2020 Top 100 Counties and Cities in Western China".

Geographical Position

Bobai County is located in the southeast of Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, adjacent to Luchuan County to the east, Lianjiang City of Guangdong Province to the southeast, Hepu County of Beihai City to the south, and Pubei County of Qinzhou City to the west. It borders Fumian District of Yulin City to the north; The total area of the county is 3835 square kilometers, between 109°38′-110°17′ east longitude and 21°38′-22°28′ north latitude.

Xingye County

Xingye has a beautiful ecological environment, and there are national AAAA-level Lufeng Mountain Scenic Area, known as the "first rock in Lingnan", the site of Li Zongrentunbing's third-level succession, Pang Village Qing Dynasty Residential Group, Xingye County Dashui Tourism Resort and other tourist landscapes, is the autonomous region level "all-area tourism" creation county. The annual slaughter volume of broiler chickens in the county accounts for one-seventh of Guangxi, and is known as the "hometown of three yellow chickens".

Honorary Title

  • National Agricultural Standardized Planting Demonstration Zone
  • The hometown of Chinese poetry
  • Forest County, Guangxi
  • Autonomous region investment attraction project large-scale cashing work demonstration county

Geographical Position

Xingye County is located in the southeast of Guangxi, northwest of Yulin City, bordered by Beiliu City to the east, Yuzhou District to the south, Guiping City to the northeast, Gangnan District of Guigang City to the northwest, and Pubei County to the southwest. It is between 22°33′-23°02′ north latitude and 109°39′-110°14′ east longitude, with a total area of 1468.38 square kilometers.
