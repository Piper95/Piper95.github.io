Deeply study and implement the spirit of General Secretary Xi Jinping's "1 July" important speech and strive to write a new chapter in the magnificent Yulin of socialism with Chinese characteristics in the new era

Yulin News Network - Yulin Daily News (reporter Guan Wei) July 5, Yulin City to celebrate the 100th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of China symposium held.Further emancipate the mind, reform and innovation, expand the opening up, take responsibility and work hard, and make unremitting efforts to write a new chapter in the magnificent Yulin of Guangxi under socialism with Chinese characteristics in the new era.Mayor Bai Songtao presided over the symposium.



At the symposium, Peng Yansen, national outstanding Communist Party representative and director of Rongxian Animal Disease Prevention and Control Center; Wu Zhiying, national Outstanding Party Affairs representative and deputy secretary of the Municipal Small Area Special Party Committee and secretary of the Party Branch of Chinese Medicinal Materials Market; Zeng Donghua, Party history representative and director of the Party History Office of Yuzhou District Committee; Yang Zhengchuan, representative of Democratic parties, director of the Municipal Medical Security Bureau and chairman of the Yulin Municipal Committee of Minjian;Liu Qi, representative of rural revitalization workers and main person in charge of Beiliu Municipal Committee; Chen Biao, representative of poverty alleviation workers and Secretary of the Party Committee of Jiangning Town, Bobai County; Xu Yanni, worker representative and chief of the New product trial-production assembly section of the Process Engineering Department of Yuchai Machinery Co., LTD.; Wei Qingmei, Youth representative, nurse of Intensive Care Medicine Department of the City Red Cross Hospital and Secretary of the Youth League Branch;

First of all, on behalf of the Municipal Party Committee, Mo Hua extended high respect to the revolutionary pioneers, revolutionary martyrs and the members of the Chinese Communist Party who have made sacrifices for the revolution, construction, reform and development of Yulin, and extended festive greetings to the Party organizations at all levels and the Communist Party members who have fought on all fronts in Yulin.

It is a program of action that guides us to comprehensively build a great modern socialist country and realize the Chinese Dream of great national renewal.Deeply understand that our Party has united and led the people in liberating their minds and forging ahead with determination to create great achievements in reform, opening up and socialist modernization; deeply understand that our Party has united and led the people in forging ahead with confidence and integrity and creating great achievements in creating socialism with Chinese characteristics for a new era; Strive to write a new chapter in the magnificent Guangxi Yulin of socialism with Chinese characteristics in the new era, and contribute Yulin strength to the great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation.

"On the new journey, I will lead the vast number of agricultural technology promoters to voluntarily integrate into the overall situation of rural revitalization strategy, take the 'field' as the main front and the main battlefield, strive to cultivate more technical personnel and local talents, and write the latest and most beautiful chapter of agricultural development on the vast land of China." The national outstanding Communist Party member, the national top ten agricultural technology promotion model, Rong County animal disease Prevention and control Center director Peng Yansen said.

"I will be in line with the half 90 miles of soberish hard work, to improve the organizational force of rural grass-roots Party organizations as the focus, as a good rural revitalization of the 'guide', with new technology, new technology, new standards to expand and strengthen the 'Hakka longan' characteristic industry, to promote the formation of branch leadership, party member demonstration, people driven by the rich model, let the villagers' life sesame blossom high."Li Chunyan, secretary of the Party branch and head of the village committee in Fengshan Town, Bobai County, said the country's "most beautiful retired soldiers".

For the city to build "four strong, two districts and one United States" two Bay pilot zone, write a new chapter in the new era of socialism with Chinese characteristics magnificent Guangxi Yulin.

"It is a fine tradition and valuable experience of our Party to make resolutions on historical issues at important historical juncture and sum up the Party's historical achievements and experience in a down-to-earth manner."Huang Stucco, National outstanding Communist Party Member, representative to the 19th CPC National Congress and deputy branch leader of the Criminal Investigation Detachment of Yulin Public Security Bureau, said that the Resolution of the CPC Central Committee on the Major Achievements and Historical Experience of the Party's Centennial Struggle approved by the Plenum was a political declaration of the Chinese Communists in the new era to remember the mission of their original aspiration, adhere to and develop socialism with Chinese characteristics."I will continue to perform my sacred duties as a delegate to the 19th National Congress of the Communist Party of China. I will continue to treat the trivial matters of the people as my major affairs. I will start with matters that the people care about and that satisfy them, so that they can feel that fairness and justice are at hand in every case and incident, and contribute to the building of a safer China and a China under the rule of law at a higher level."

"The resolution adopted by the plenary session made me feel that the construction industry and the construction of great projects have the same wonderful.To lead the development of the construction industry in the right direction, we must adhere to the leadership of the Party, give full play to the leading role of the Party committee in the construction industry, let the Party flag fly high on the construction site, build ingenuity with the Party heart, and build more projects that can stand the test of history and the people."Fu Mian District Yulin City construction industry Party secretary Tang Mengchun said.

The achievement of a hundred years of brilliant, can not be separated from the relay and responsibility of countless cadres.Huang Ting, member of the Standing Committee of Fumian District Party Committee and Minister of the Organization Department, said that on the way to the second centenary goal, they will carry out the "stick project" of young cadres deeply, push more young cadres to go to the front line to struggle through wind and rain, strengthen their muscles and bones, build their souls, and forge a young fresh force that is fearless of changes, brave to break the situation and good at opening new situations.
