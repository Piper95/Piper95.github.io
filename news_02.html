The Puqing Expressway Tunnel Project ushered in a "good start" - the right line of the Xianyuan Tunnel was successfully completed

Located in Santan Town, Bobai County, Xiandao Tunnel is the only girder passage of the Tianchishan Special Bridge of the Puqing Expressway, and "shoulders" the important mission of the construction period of the Tianchishan Special Bridge.

According to reports, the Xiandao Tunnel is located in Santan Town, Bobai County, which is the only girder passage of the Tianchishan Special Bridge of the Puqing Expressway Difficult Project, and "shoulders" the important mission of ensuring the completion of the construction period of the Tianchishan Special Bridge on schedule. The tunnel is a two-hole split tunnel, with a total length of 1090m on the left and 1105m on the right. The tunnel is designed for a driving speed of 120km/h, a net width of 13.75m and a net height of 5.0m. The left and right holes of the tunnel entrance and the right exit are all end-wall doors, and the left exit is biased door, with 2 pedestrian crossholes and 1 vehicular crosshole.

"The construction of ancillary works in the cave will be carried out gradually, and it is expected to be completed in May this year." Deng Wenke, the construction supervisor of the entrance of the Xianyuan Tunnel of the fifth bid section of Puqing Expressway, introduced that due to the impact of the line optimization plan, the actual start time of the Xiandao Tunnel was half a year later than the original plan. With the efforts of all personnel for more than a year, the right line of the Xianyuan Tunnel was successfully completed.
