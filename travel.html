Yuntian Palace

National AAAALevel

No.461, Jiangbin Road, Yulin City

Yuntian Culture City is the abbreviation of "Guangxi Yulin Yuntian Folk Culture World", also known as "Yuntian Palace". According to the records, the series of events from the settlement to the construction of the Yuntian Palace to the back are full of mysterious and unspeakable colors.

The settlement is a mystery, some say that the Yulin Taiwanese businessman escaped a calamity here during the Cultural Revolution, so he thought this place was a treasure, and built a building here to bring good luck. Others say that the Taiwanese businessman hired an airplane and saw this treasure in the sky. It is also said that the Taiwanese businessmen were entrusted with dreams by the immortal Buddha, saying that Yulin is the place with the strongest magnetic field in the world and is a blessed place that can bring good luck to people. After arriving in Yulin, these Taiwanese businessmen first saw a place shaped like a mushroom and reed in Dongming Village on the outskirts of the city, near the highway and the South Stream River. It is also known as Yuntian Palace. At the early stage of construction, the foundation was drilled to 36 meters and then the foundation could not go down any more. Later, the whole foundation was surveyed to be a whole piece of raw stone grindstone comparable to diamond, just like a cloud floating under the center of the earth, holding up the lofty palace, so it got the name "Yuntian". Now there are still a small number of foundation stones preserved in Yuntian Palace, which is extremely valuable. There are rumors that this is a magical and excellent "gold line hanging gourd" feng shui treasure, once according to have a long life! Because of its strong mysterious color and its more than the Potala Palace majestic ancient palace building and is commonly known as the "Cloud Palace" by the locals.

So a Guangxi Potala Palace called, is floating in the heart of the earth on the clouds, China's largest single giant art palace, Southeast Asia's largest high-altitude bronze statue, China's most valuable cultural heritage, Guangxi's most mysterious cultural world, was born!

Tongshi Ridge

National AAAALevel

Fengcun Road, Minan Town, Beiliu, Yulin City

Tong Shi Ling is divided into two mountains, the size of the Gui River from the south to the north to spare the front of the mountain, the mountain layer change towering show, lofty and spectacular, the stone are purple and copper color, some independent, skeletal; some pale, majestic. These breathtaking strange peaks and rocks, a series of different shapes, a variety of postures, lifelike.

Copperstone Ridge is famous for its "three great things": firstly, it is the world's largest copper drum casting place and the place where coins were minted for generations; secondly, it has a symbiosis of karst and Danxia landforms; thirdly, the Temple of Heavenly God and Earthly Mother on the mountain is said to have been formed naturally since Pan Gu opened up the sky and opened up the earth.

The peculiar stone scenery is mostly concentrated around the foot of the mountain. Darong Mountain

National AAAALevel

Yulin Beiliu City, Dairongshan Forestry Field within

Darong Mountain is a famous historical mountain. Gaozu of the later Han Dynasty, who became the emperor in present-day Guangzhou in 917, made Dairong Mountain the "Western Mountain of the South" in the same way as the Central Plains. The park has four major natural landscapes, including the majestic mountain landscape, the rich and colorful forest vegetation landscape, the fascinating water landscape and the unpredictable meteorological landscape. Especially because the park is rich in forest and water, it is very rainy and foggy, and it is amazing to see the changes of clouds and fog, not to mention the fascinating scenery of the high mountain and flat lake formed by Lianchi, Lianhu and Tianhu, which are more than 800 meters above sea level.

Darong Mountain National Forest Park is 50 square kilometers in circumference, with majestic mountains, numerous streams, rich water bodies, forest vegetation coverage of more than 95%, and beautiful and charming natural scenery. Longmen Scenic Area, Lotus Scenic Area and Tian Lai Scenic Area have their own characteristics, which are woven into a beautiful picture of mountain flowers in spring, lush forests in summer, wild fruits in golden autumn, and a transparent ice scene in winter. -European scenery, is a high quality original ecological tourism, vacation and health resort.

Zhenwu Pavilion

National AAAALevel

No.57, Dongwai Street, Rong County, Yulin City

Jingliu Terrace Zhenwu Pavilion, a Chinese Taoist palace building, was used to worship Emperor Zhenwu to suppress fire. Its structure is of three-story eaves, hutted roof, and pierced bucket frame, with a height of 13.2 meters, a width of 13.8 meters, a depth of 11.2 meters, and a total area of 168 square meters. The structure is made up of nearly 3,000 wooden elements of different sizes, with mortise and tenon holes cut into them, diagonally pierced and straight set, in tandem, supporting each other and restraining each other, without a single nail or rivet, and without a single iron member, forming a solid unity by the principle of "lever structure".

Jinglutai Zhenwu Pavilion is known as the "South of Heaven Masterpiece", together with Yueyanglou, Huanghelou and Tengwangge, it is also the only one of the four famous buildings that has not been rebuilt and has been preserved intact. On February 23, 1982, Jinglutai Zhenwu Pavilion was announced as the second batch of national key cultural relics protection units by the State Council of the People's Republic of China, and on August 28, 2017, Jinglutai Zhenwu Pavilion was approved as a national 4A-level tourist attraction.
